About Me

Hi, I'm Alyssa!

I'm a passionate graphic design student with a keen interest in UX/UI design, package design, and advertising! I am a proud graduate of the University of Arkansas in Fort Smith. Throughout my degree program in Graphic Design, I've developed a strong foundation in visual communication and found a specific niche in advertisement, branding, and web design. I have developed skills among a variety different areas such as photography, video editing, and coding as well. Designing and creating is something I am very passionate about I take pride in my designs, and love learning new skills and taking on projects that will help me grow as a designer. I am looking forward to expanding my skills and knowledge towards my design career, and am incredibly excited to see where it takes me!
